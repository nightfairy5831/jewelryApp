# jewelry app
Mobile app (iOS + Android) for jewelry sales in Brazil with:
- Super Admin control over sellers and products
- Automatic gold price updates every 4 hours (in Brazilian Real - BRL)
- Automated customer emails (Portuguese language support)
- 3D product viewer, Video product viewer, Image viewer
- Complete e-commerce features (sales and order management, inventory and shipping management)
- Brazilian payment methods (PIX, Credit Cards)
